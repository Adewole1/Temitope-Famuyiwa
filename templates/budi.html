Budi

Find events you love and people to go with

OVERVIEW

Budi is a social app that helps people find events they love and also recommends people who share similar interests to go with.

Role Team Tools Timeline
Product Designer Individual Figma 3 weeks
UX Researcher

PROBLEM

Sometimes people want to go to events they enjoy like a concert museum exhibits, career fairs, movies, etc. but can't find people with similar interests to go with.

These people might not want to experience the event alone, so they forego the oppportunity.

DESIGN CHALLENGE

How might we help people who like to go to events discover unique events and people to enjoy them with?

SOLUTION

The goal of Budi is to make it possible and easy for people to find events easily and for one or more persons to go to the event with them.

This gives them the opportunity to make friends and spend time with people and create the best possible experience for these Users.

Interests and Preference

With the simple onboarding process, users are able to set their profile in minutes to get the best event recommendations.

Users are able to select at least three categories or preferences to enable Budi to find the best possible events that suit users.

Event Recommendations

The home page also showcases recommendations and upcoming events based on the user's location and interests.

Users are able to view the details of events (location, time, about, etc.) and people who are going to these similar events. they are also able to save events that interests them.

Comments

Users are able to make comments to enhance the user experience and keep users engaged and social within the application.

Inapp chat & messaging

Users are able to get connected going to similar events. How is this possible? On Budi, people going to similar events are displayed so as to enable other users like them to find them.

These users are able to view their profile (Name, Location, Bio, Interests) and chat with them to help them get familiar with them.

Sharing events

Users are able to share events with friends within their social network that might want to tag along.

Push notifications

These users are able to view their profile (Name, Location, Bio, Interests) and chat with them to help them get familiar with them.

#1 USER Research

I interviewed 5 peole who go to events on their needs, pain points, and how they currently find people to go to an event with. I also collected 17 responses in an online survey for my research.

And based on the responses here are the key insights;

  • All respondents said that they prefer to join events offline
  • Most people get to kmow about events throigh their families, friends, and social hangouts. Some people get to kmow about events from social media platforms.
  • Most of the reapondents said they prefer to go to events with people
  • They prefer to go with someone they are familiar with and can be comfortable around.
  • People would want to know about the people they are going to the event with.

DESIGN OPPORTUNITY

We identified opportunities to build a product with a focus on people finding events they enjoy, helping them find people with similar interests to go with, making friends, and building relationships.
